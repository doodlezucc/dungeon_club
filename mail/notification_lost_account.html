Recovery of your Account
Hi there!
I'm doodlezucc, developer of the virtual tabletop Dungeon Club. If I'm not mistaken, you've registered an account in the last three weeks and are affected by a certain software bug.
Your account got unregistered on the server by accident. All your campaigns are alive and well! However, they're currently in an orphan state, with no assigned game owner. They're basically in limbo.
It's no big hassle, really: to regain access to your account and campaigns, you can either reset your password on the website or re-register the account at this link:
$URL
Some details on what happened:
An unhandled error in the server code caused the periodic auto-save to stop auto-saving the account database. The same database is also used to store campaign ownership.
Early in the morning today, the server had apparently shut down unexpectedly. Due to the last backup dating back to October 22nd, all account related changes between the auto-save and the shutdown had been undone. To be clear, this "memory gap" did not revert the actual contents of any campaign, only their individual assigned owners.
By running a bunch of simulations on the pseudonymized server logs and by making a few educated guesses, relationships between accounts and created campaigns affected by the memory gap could eventually be established and were restored in the database.
If you find any campaigns missing from your account, please don't hesitate to reach out by replying to this email or joining the community Discord server.
Sorry for any inconveniences the bug might have caused.
